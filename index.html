Malleefowl

Malleefowl is a house concert series for improvised, experimental and electro-acoustic music in Perth, Western Australia, programming ambitious local performers alongside compositions and electronic pieces from artists worldwide.

Malleefowl lives at the Meltham Cultural Precinct. The address can be obtained by RSVPing to our gmail address below. The venue has a 14cm step at the doorway to access the house or bathrooms, but concerts can be held in the garden when the weather's good. Get in touch with any accessibility concerns.
