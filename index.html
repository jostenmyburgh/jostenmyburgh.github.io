Malleefowl

Malleefowl is a tiny concert series for improvised, experimental and electro-acoustic music in Boorloo/Perth, Western Australia, programming ambitious local performers and occasional visiting artists.

Concerts are pay-what-you-want with all money going to the artists. Dinner is provided for performers and audience to share afterwards, for which donations are appreciated to cover costs.

Malleefowl lives at the Meltham Cultural Precinct. The address of the venue will be sent when you RSVP.

When the weather is good, Malleefowl happens outdoors in the garden, which is ramp accessible. There is a 14cm step at the doorway to access the house or bathrooms. Please get in touch with any accessibility concerns.

Sign up to the mailing list for news on upcoming concerts.

Malleefowl acknowledges the Whadjuk people of the Noongar nation as the custodians of the land on which we are based.
