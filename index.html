Malleefowl

Malleefowl is a tiny concert series for improvised, experimental and electro-acoustic music in Boorloo/Perth, Western Australia, programming ambitious local performers and occasional visiting artists.

Malleefowl lives at the Meltham Cultural Precinct. The address will be sent to you once you RSVP to a concert via our gmail address below. The venue has a 14cm step at the doorway to access the house or bathrooms, but concerts can be held in the garden when the weather's good. Get in touch with any accessibility concerns.

Sign up to the mailing list for news on upcoming concerts.

Malleefowl acknowledges the Whadjuk people of the Noongar nation as the custodians of the land on which we are based.
