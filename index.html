Malleefowl

Malleefowl is a concert series for experimental music in Boorloo on Whadjuk Noongar country (Perth, Western Australia). Performances at Malleefowl draw from a wide-range of approaches but share a playful, exploratory approach to music-making, and a high level of care and attention to detail. Our programs involve work by ambitious local artists as well as scores & electro-acoustic works by contemporary composers internationally. Malleefowl concerts are recorded professionally and published on this website.

Sign up to the mailing list for news on upcoming concerts.

Mallee Fowl Music acknowledges the Whadjuk people of the Noongar nation as the custodians of the land on which we are based.
